BUSINESS LAW

The lawyers at Simonds, Winslow, Willis & Abbott represent business clients in all aspects of relevant law, including entity formation, management, development, operations, finance, asset transactions, and mergers and acquisitions.

We are also frequently called upon for advice relating to business contracts and other transactions, and when necessary, representation in disputes that may arise in the ever-shifting commercial environment. Our added expertise in estate planning and taxation allow us to offer other essential legal services required by business owners.

Our business law clients range from start-up enterprises to multi-million dollar corporations, and are involved in a wide variety of industries, including real estate, health care, construction, manufacturing, and financial and professional services.

The business lawyers at Simonds, Winslow strive to provide their clients with the highest quality legal representation, delivered promptly and efficiently, while maintaining the cost effectiveness that business clients expect and deserve from their legal counsel.
