DIRECTIONS TO THE BOSTON OFFICE

Please note: The nearest public parking is available next door at 75 State Street (parking garage entrance on Broad Street). Click on Map for other garages and general directions.

By Public Transportation (MBTA):

Red Line to Downtown Crossing. Switch to Orange Line towards Oak Grove. Exit at State Street. Follow signs to Blue Line and then signs to exit at Old State House. (Old State House is at the intersection of Devonshire, State, and Congress Streets). Cross Congress Street and go right one block past Exchange Place to #50.

Orange Line to State Street station. Follow signs to Blue Line and then signs to exit at Old State House. (Old State House is at the intersection of Devonshire, State, and Congress Streets). Cross Congress Street and go right one block past Exchange Place to #50.

Green Line to Government Center. At exit, turn right to Court Street. Turn left on Court and follow to State Street (at Old State House). Cross Congress Street and go right one block past Exchange Place to #50.

Blue Line to State Street. Take the Old State House exit. Cross Congress Street and go right one block past Exchange Place to #50.

By Train (Amtrak/South Station):
After arriving at Boston's South Station via Amtrak. A comfortable 10-minute walk, or follow the MBTA Red Line instructions above.

By Car:
From Points North:

  1. Take I-93 South to exit 24A, Government Center
  2. Get in the left lane and follow signs for Aquarium/Financial District
  3. At the end of exit, bear left and proceed onto Atlantic Avenue southbound
  4. Take first right onto State Street
  5. Take left onto Broad Street and make right into the 75 State Street garage
  6. Take elevator to lobby and exit onto Kilby Street (by Rebecca's Café) to rear entrance of 50 Congress Street across the street on left.

From Points South:

  1. Take I-93 North into the Central Artery tunnel
  2. Take exit 23, Government Center
  3. Stay in the left lane, turning left onto North Street at the end of the ramp (follow signs toward Government Center)
  4. Take left onto Congress Street
  5. Follow Congress Street past State Street to Post Office Square
  6. Take left onto Milk Street
  7. Continue on Milk Street until you reach Broad Street
  8. Take left onto Broad Street
  9. Take left into 75 State Street garage (just before State Street)
  10. Take elevator to lobby and exit onto Kilby Street (by Rebecca's Café) to rear entrance of 50 Congress Street across the street on left.

From Points West:

  1. Take Mass Pike (I-90) to I-93 North
  2. Take exit 23, Government Center
  3. Stay in the left lane, turning left onto North Street at the end of the ramp (follow signs toward Government Center)
  4. Take left onto Congress Street
  5. Follow Congress Street past State Street to Post Office Square
  6. Take left onto Milk Street
  7. Continue on Milk Street until you reach Broad Street
  8. Take left onto Broad Street
  9. Take left into 75 State Street garage (just before State Street)
  10. Take elevator to lobby and exit onto Kilby Street (by Rebecca's Café) to rear entrance of 50 Congress Street across the street on left.

From Logan Airport:

  • Follow signs for the Ted Williams Tunnel/Mass Pike/I-90 West
  • Take the Ted Williams Tunnel to the Mass Pike (I-90) West
  • Use Exit 24-25 after leaving the Ted Williams Tunnel
  • The exit ramp runs parallel to I-90 for approximately 1/2 mile until it enters the northbound Central Artery tunnel
  • Proceed north about 1/2 mile to exit 23, Government Center
  • Take left onto North Street (follow signs toward Government Center)
  • Take left onto Congress Street
  • Follow Congress Street past State Street to Post Office Square
  • Take left onto Milk Street
  • Continue on Milk Street until you reach Broad Street
  • Take left onto Broad Street
  • Take left into 75 State Street garage (just before State Street)
  • Take elevator to lobby and exit onto Kilby Street (by Rebecca's Café) to rear entrance of 50 Congress Street across the street on left.
